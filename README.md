Inspired by: 
- **Cas & Simmons (2018)**  
  Subaqueous–subaerial eruption dynamics.  
  DOI: https://doi.org/10.3389/feart.2018.00198

- **Spear (1993)**  
  *Metamorphic Phase Equilibria and P–T–t Paths* — foundational synthesis of crustal heat production and metamorphic trajectories.  
  Resource: https://minsocam.org

- **Hoinkes et al. (2005)**  
  Classification of geothermal gradients across subduction, collision, and magmatic arc settings.
